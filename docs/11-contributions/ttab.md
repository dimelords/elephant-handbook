## Background

TT is Sweden's leading news agency, founded in 1921. When Naviga acquired TT, the Elephant platform became part of Naviga's editorial systems portfolio, with TT providing the organizational presence for the open-source community.

## TT as Part of Naviga

While TT operates the open-source presence, it's important to understand:

- **Naviga** is the architect and primary developer
- **TT** provides the organizational front for open source
- Both are part of the same corporate structure
- Both are committed to open source success

This structure allows:
- **Naviga** to focus on development and enterprise features
- **TT** to focus on community engagement and open source management
- **Community** to have clear points of contact
